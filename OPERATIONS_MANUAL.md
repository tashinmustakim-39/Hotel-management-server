### 4. Inventory Management Protocol
Staff must use the \`inv3ntoryController.js\` to log any displaced assets.
* **Thresholds:** Any item moved > 50cm from its "Ideal State" triggers a level 2 alert.
* **Reconciliation:** All missing items must be reconciled before 11:59 PM.
* **Auditing:** Weekly physical audits must match the \`assetRegistry.json\`.

### 5. Detailed Emergency Protocols
In the event of a "Hospitality Entropy" spike (detected by \`idealController.js\`):

#### Phase A: Detection
1. Monitor the console for "Entropy Variance > 0.05".
2. Isolate the affected room index in the registry.

#### Phase B: Mitigation
1. Calmly direct guests to the Virtual Reality buffer zone.
2. Reset the spatial math utility in the backend.
3. Perform a hard-reset of the Inventory Nexus.

#### Phase C: Recovery
1. Recalibrate all sensors in the affected zone.
2. Update the system codes to prevent recurring variance.
